## What It Solves

Finding the right AI tool for any task — image generation, voice cloning, video creation, content writing, coding, or orchestration — is overwhelming. Krittimat is a curated, structured hub that maps every major AI tool to its use case, so you spend less time searching and more time creating.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Tool Directory**: Categorized by task — image, video, voice, writing, code, and more.
- **Prompt Library**: Practical, real-world prompts optimized for each tool.
- **LLM Era Coverage**: Covers the full AI toolchain from foundation models to orchestration.
- **Assamese-Friendly**: Built with accessibility for Assamese users in mind.

## Who Is It For

Creators, students, freelancers, and anyone curious about AI tools who wants a reliable, up-to-date reference without wading through hype.
