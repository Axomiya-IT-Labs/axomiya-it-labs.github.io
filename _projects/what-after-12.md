## What It Solves

Class 12 students in Assam face a critical decision gap. Structured career counseling in Assamese is practically non-existent in semi-urban and rural areas. This project provides an AI-guided assistant that explains streams, scholarships, local colleges, and job pathways in plain Assamese.

## Key Features

- **Assamese-First Interface**: Full localized experience with optional English toggle.
- **AI Recommendation Engine**: Filters diplomas, degree courses, and vocational paths based on student interest and budget.
- **Verified Local Database**: Curated catalog of Assam universities, polytechnics, and state scholarships.

## Who Is It For

Class 10 & 12 students, parents, high school teachers, and career counselors in Assam.
